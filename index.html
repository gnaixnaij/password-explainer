💡 Tips

  • Length beats complexity — 16+ random characters is stronger than "P@ssw0rd!"
  • Dictionary words with substitutions (P@ssw0rd) are still cracked in seconds
  • Use a password manager and generate 20+ character random passwords
  • Enable 2FA everywhere — it stops the attacks that cracking doesn't
